#Pre-Virtualization World:	
		   ----App----
	    -------Bins/Libs---------
	----Host Operating System------
------------Physical Server---------------

Problems:	- Huge Cost
		- Slow Deployment
		- Hard to Migrate
 		- High CAPEX and OPEX

#Hypervisor-based Virtualization


	 App1			 App2			 App3
	Bins/Libs		Bins/Libs		Bins/Libs
	Guest OS		Guest OS		Guest OS
--------------------------------Hypervisor-------------------------
-------------------------Host Operating System---------------------
----------------------------Physical Server------------------------

Benefits:
- Cost Efficient
- Easy to scale

Limitations:
- Kernel Resource Duplication
- Application Portability issue
